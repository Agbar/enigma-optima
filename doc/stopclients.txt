NAME
        stopclients  -  stop client(s) under the supervision of
                        task scheduler.


SYNOPSIS  
        stopclients


DESCRIPTION
        You can manually stop clients under the supervision of the task
        scheduler using stopclients.

        Use startclients to start them again.


SEE ALSO
        startclients(1)
