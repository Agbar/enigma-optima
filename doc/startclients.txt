NAME
        startclients  -  restart client(s) under the supervision of
                         task scheduler.


SYNOPSIS  
        startclients


DESCRIPTION
        Restart clients previously stopped by stopclients.

        For starting the clients in the foreground, use fgstart.


SEE ALSO
        stopclients(1), fgstart(1)
