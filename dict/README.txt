
Format of log-ngram dictionaries, single line (read mkdict.py for details):
===========================================================================

<ngram> <TAB> <logarithm of ngram frequency in sample text converted to int>


Sources for provided dictionaries:
==================================

00bigr.gen and 00trigr.gen have been compiled from World War I Army Reports.

00bigr.cur and 00trigr.cur have been compiled from original World War II
Naval Messages.
